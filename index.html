A Crisis in Haiti

Stand with us to bring hope and lasting change.

The Crisis

Haiti has faced monumental challenges—from the catastrophic 2010 earthquake to the turmoil following the 2021 presidential assassination.

Today, over 80% of Port Au Princé is under siege, with thousands displaced and vital services in jeopardy. Haiti is facing a deepening medical and humanitarian crisis—hospitals are closing, cholera is spreading, and over a million people are displaced by violence. With rising hunger and collapsing health services, urgent action is needed.

The Impact

This crisis isn’t just about numbers—it’s about real lives disrupted by violence, poverty, and despair.

Communities are losing hope, and urgent action is needed to rebuild and restore dignity.

Our Vision

We Kids United SJA is committed to transforming crisis into opportunity. Through sustainable initiatives, we aim to rebuild Haiti’s healthcare system and empower its economy.

Join us in our mission to restore hope and create a brighter future for Haiti.

The Reality

A devastating earthquake, political upheaval, and gang violence have left Haiti in dire need.

The Numbers

Over 80% of Port Au Princé under siege and 700,000+ displaced—each number tells a story of struggle.

Our Call to Action

Your support can help rebuild communities, restore healthcare, and renew hope in a nation desperate for change.
