Our Initiatives

MYI MUN Korea 4.0

In collaboration with MyiMUN_Korea, we host MYI MUN Korea 4.0 to raise awareness and funds by engaging young minds on the Haitian crisis.

Charity Movie Night

Partnering with SJA Roots and Shoots, our Charity Movie Night unites the community for an evening of film and conversation, all to support sustainable projects in Haiti.

Haiti Poster Contest

Our poster contest challenges creative minds to capture Haiti’s resilience and plight—raising both awareness and crucial funds.

Medical Devices Donation

In partnership with university hospitals, we donate essential medical devices directly to Haiti, strengthening its healthcare system one life at a time.
