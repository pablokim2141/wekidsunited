Our Mission & The Challenge

"Transforming Crisis into Opportunity"

At We Kids United SJA, our mission is to turn the devastation in Haiti into a catalyst for sustainable change. We strive to rebuild healthcare systems and empower communities through long-term, community-driven initiatives.

The Crisis in Haiti

From the catastrophic 2010 earthquake to the political turmoil following the 2021 presidential assassination, Haiti has faced relentless challenges. With over 80% of Port Au Princé under gang control, the nation’s people are in desperate need of support.

Statistic: Over 700,000 people have been displaced from their homes.
